Introduction to Moving Water at Shepperton Weir

Details
By Niki Norman

Meet at the clubhouse at 10.00am. Remember to bring clothes to wear on the water and a set of dry clothes to change into, a towel, lunch, snacks and drinks. Hopefully you will also need sunscreen! We aim be back at the clubhouse by 5.00pm.

This is this the first of a series of trips to introduce paddlers to moving water. We are going to start with practising breaking in and out and ferry gliding. While this is aimed at introducing paddlers to moving water, anyone wanting to brush up on their skills in gentle moving water is welcome to join us.

Please let me know you if you are planning to come so that we can sort out transport arrangements, either on the forum or by emailing me. 

This trip depends on the river level being low so let's hope that we have a dry week.
