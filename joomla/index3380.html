Paddlesport Activity Assistant

Details
By Mark Corti

The Club is really keen to support our more-experienced members who are leading (or would like to lead) paddles for our newer members.  Organised mostly through the MCC2021 WhatsApp group (and always on the website), these paddles have been the mainstay in keeping our newer members on the water throughout lockdown.

Jacob & Lee have been working to put together a special Maidstone Canoe Club program to recognise these paddlers whohave been organising trips.  The new British Canoeing Paddlesport Activity Assistant qualification is the ideal way to do this.  It's a Club-specific program which takes into account all your existing experience, but also offers full training and support to less-confident aspiring paddle leaders.  The aim is that in future, all of these trips will be led by a qualified PAA (or other river leader / coach).  To this end, Jacob & Lee are going to run some sessions supporting this - to help them get organised, please register your interest using this questionnaire.  Thanks!

The Newsletter Returns ...

Details
By Mark Corti

Like a bear emerging blinking from his cave after a long hibernation, the newsletter is back!  Or at least, it will be if you lovely people tell me what you've been up to.  Gear reviews, paddling stories, thoughts on courses you've been on (online or IRL), club news, book reviews, poems - everything very welcome, and doubly so if it comes with photos!

The deadline is May 20th, and you can send it to the usual address - newsletter@maidstonecanoeclub.net. Thanks in advance - the newsletter can't happen without you!

Covid Update

Details
By Catherine Dacey

Monday 12 April Covid Update

From Monday 12 April, members will be able to use the changing rooms, gym area and kitchen.

The full details of use are in the Covid 19 information Tab on the left of this screen.

Please use the token system for access to the relevant area, and remember to sanitise, and wear a face covering while in any of the buildings.

When using the gym, please open the windows and close them when you leave.

Back on the water - Explore Award

Details
By Mark Corti

We're starting the season off with a slew of Explore Award courses to get us back in the swing of things!  These are the next step from the beginner course / Discover Award, and focus on building the skills and knowledge to take trips on rivers both local and further afield.  (The Explore Award is now required if you want to have a key for the boat shed).  If you're a bit rusty, it's also a great way to refresh your skills and hopefully learn some new ones too!

Courses are running on Monday evenings, Tuesday evenings, and a couple of Saturdays and Sundays also.
