A weekly paddle with the emphasis on fun while learning new skills across different craft. A high chance of getting wet!!!

Open to all Club members from 8 - 16, our Junior Paddle Club is all about having fun on the water!  Make new friends, paddle new boats, get awesome at kayaking - all at once!

If this is your first time:

  • Please fill out the form below for your child.
  • There is a £45 once-off training fee, payable online in advance.  
  • If your child enjoys the training (they all do!) and wants to continue paddling with us, they will need to become a member of both British Canoeing (£29 annually) and Maidstone Canoe Club (£36 annually).  After that, all sessions are free!

If your child has already completed the training:

  • Just turn up and have fun!

 

All sessions are led by our fully-qualified and insured coaches.  All our coaches and leaders hold an up-to-date DBS check.  We provide all boats, paddles and safety equipment.

For suggestions on what to wear, please see this page.
