About Us

  • Print

We are a family friendly club for all those who enjoy marathon and sprint racing, river touring, white water,sea touring or just a leisurely paddle.

Our clubhouse is situated on the banks of the River Medway near to the centre of Maidstone, the County Town of Kent, UK.

New members are always welcome. You do not need to have had previous experience t come and have a go - we can provide beginners with the equipment, facilities and expertise to help you enjoy a great sport. Paddling a canoe or kayak is a great way to keep fit, while enjoying the company of others and getting a different view of the countryside.   Our regular paddles are on Sundays & Wednesdays - please read our Come & Try pages and then check the club calendar for when is the best time to come along.
