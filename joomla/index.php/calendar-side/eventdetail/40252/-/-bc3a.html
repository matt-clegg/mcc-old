Saturday 01 July 2023, 21:00 - 23:30
      

The 1st full moon after midsummer eve is on Monday July 3rd but hopefully we will get to see a reasonably full moon on this  Saturday evening peer paddle from Yalding back to the club (although  if last year's moonlight paddle repeats itself then this could be a 'spot the moonlight'  rather than a moonlight paddle!!)

....but either way we will hopefully have a lovely midsummer evening paddle with good weather.

Meet at the club to load boats at 8.20pm, or at Yalding  to be on the water for 9pm.  I would anticipate getting back to the club about 11.30pm

Please message  me directly via MCC 2023  if you are interested in coming along or have any questions about the paddle and I will set up a WhatsApp group to sort  transport. 
Contact Trish Cadman

Disclaimer: all our trips are run by club members like you, and unless specifically described otherwise are peer paddles.  If you think that you would benefit from the support of a formal river leader or coach, please contact the trip organiser in advance - we can usually arrange something!  Otherwise you should be confident that your own ability matches the trip as planned.  If your trip is being led by someone - a more experienced paddler, river leader or a coach - it is your responsibility to mention any medical or other issues which may arise during the trip.
