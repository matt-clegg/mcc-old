Friday 22 July 2016, 15:00
To Sunday 24 July 2016
      

Once again we will be running the sea paddling trip based at  http://www.redcliffefarmcamping.co.uk/ near Wareham. The plan is to paddle from Poole Harbour back to the campsite, on the River Frome, on Saturday and out to Old Harry Rock off Studland Beach on Sunday. As with all sea paddling it is dependent on the weather conditions, not too windy. This is suitable for tourers and cross over boats as well as sea kayaks but there are two sea kayaks in the club that can be borrowed, first come first served. If you would like to go this year please get in touch so I know roughly the numbers. Given the time of year it will be best to book the campsite early so please do this yourself as they seem to worry about a large group of adults.

Tony has reserved 12 pitches on the campsite so could all those intending to go please contact me, so far I think  Tony, Jerry, Carolyn, Norman, Debbie, Michaela, Catherine, Mark,  Mike, Claudia & Phil have said they intend to go so with me that uses 12 pitches, if you would like to be added to this list please let me know, by e-mail so I don't forget.

Geoff

Disclaimer: all our trips are run by club members like you, and unless specifically described otherwise are peer paddles.  If you think that you would benefit from the support of a formal river leader or coach, please contact the trip organiser in advance - we can usually arrange something!  Otherwise you should be confident that your own ability matches the trip as planned.  If your trip is being led by someone - a more experienced paddler, river leader or a coach - it is your responsibility to mention any medical or other issues which may arise during the trip.
