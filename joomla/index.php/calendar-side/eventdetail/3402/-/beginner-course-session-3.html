Beginner's Course for Club Newcomers

Paddle with us!  If you're thinking of joining Maidstone Canoe Club and learning to paddle, you're in the right place!  This 4-week Beginner's Course is designed to give you the basic skills to control the boat.  Once you've completed the course, you'll have the confidence and abilities to join us on our trips, tours, and regular paddles on the Medway and beyond.

Please note that courses must be pre-booked.  We are no longer running "open" sessions - all novice paddlers must attend one of our Introductory Courses.  Please see this page for details.

Full details of the courses and how to get involved in paddling at the Club are available on this page - please take a look if you haven't already done so.

Our courses cost 25, and are run by our friendly qualified coaches.  They consist of four 2-hour sessions.  

Your coaches for this Course will be Mark Corti.  The dates for this course are:

Session 1 4th September
Session 2 11th September
Session 3 18th September
Session 4 25th September

Boats used for this session: open boats

If you can't attend one of the sessions, please let one of the coaches know and we'll try to fit you in another time.  Please arrive at 5:30pm - we will aim to be on the water by 6:00pm.

If you'd like to attend this course, please fill out the form below to sign up.  If you've got questions or have any problems, please contact us and we'll do our best to help.

See you on the water!

 

This course begins on Wednesday, 4th September
Location Maidstone Canoe Club, Mill Meadow, St Peter's St, Maidstone, ME16 0SN
Contact event.bookings@maidstonecanoeclub.net
