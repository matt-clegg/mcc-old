Splashing Fun in the Sun!

Our Kids Club is the way for your kids to cool off this summer - as well as burning off some energy and getting them out of the house!  Run by our fully-qualified coaches, your kids will paddle our fleet of kayaks on the beautiful River Medway, with loads of laughter, fun & games thrown in!  They'll also come away with their Paddlepower Passport Award, a nationally-recognised canoeing qualification designed specifically for kids by British Canoeing.  They'll make new friends, learn new skills, develop new confidence - all under the safe supervision of our river leaders.  And for only £35 for all 6 sessions, you can't go wrong!
