From Friday 27 November 2015
To Sunday 29 November 2015
      

Meridian are running a trip to the Upper Tees and have invited Maidstone to join them.  The plan is to paddle from High Force to Low Force, which is a fantastic grade 3/4 section of river.

Andy Garlick (who many of you will know or at least recognise) is organising the trip.  Meridian aren’t putting on any coaching or river leading per se, but they’ve got a couple of experienced paddlers who will be with the group (Jack Lamer & Hannah).

Given the river grade and no coaching, this isn’t really a trip for beginners; competence on grade 3 water and a reliable roll are probably pre-requisites, but have a chat to people who have run it for more info.  See here for more:
http://www.ukriversguidebook.co.uk/rivers/england/north-east/river-tees-high-force-to-wynch-bridge

Meridian's forum talking about this trip is here:

http://www.meridiancanoeclub.com/index.php/forum

Please contact Mark or Andy if you're planning on coming.
Location Kingsway Centre, Alston Road, Middleton-in-Teesdale, Co Durham, DL12 0UU
Contact Mark, mark@maidstonecanoeclub.net / Andy Garlick, andy.garlick@yahoo.com

Disclaimer: all our trips are run by club members like you, and unless specifically described otherwise are peer paddles.  If you think that you would benefit from the support of a formal river leader or coach, please contact the trip organiser in advance - we can usually arrange something!  Otherwise you should be confident that your own ability matches the trip as planned.  If your trip is being led by someone - a more experienced paddler, river leader or a coach - it is your responsibility to mention any medical or other issues which may arise during the trip.
