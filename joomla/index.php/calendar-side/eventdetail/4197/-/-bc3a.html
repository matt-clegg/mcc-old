From Friday 23 June 2017 -  08:00
To Sunday 25 June 2017 - 17:00
      

Touring trip to South Wales.  This will be centred on the river Wye and aimed at Touring paddlers but everyone looking for grade 1/2rivers. Are   welcome Saturday day 1 Glabury - Whitney on Wye 10 miles Sunday day 2 Ross on wye - Symonds Yat 13 miles

 Please bring with you the following

Lunch or you can buy it there.

Camping kit 1- 2 man Tent only as campsite is small

Paddle kit

Camping fee £8.00 per adult per night (£5 for under 12 years)

Money for pub  

Food for BBQ Friday night

Breakfast menu – your choice of:

Breakfast bap filled with your choice of locally sourced bacon, sausage, free range egg, fresh tomato and chestnut mushrooms plus unlimited selection of beverages incl. green tea, tea, fruit tea, hot chocolate and coffee – delivered to your tent - £5.00 each PLEASE EMAIL ME Order by Wednesday 21st June if you would like this.
Location Whitney Bridge Toll Bridge Cottage Whitney-on-Wye Herefordshire HR3 6EW
Contact lee@maidstonecanoeclub.net

Disclaimer: all our trips are run by club members like you, and unless specifically described otherwise are peer paddles.  If you think that you would benefit from the support of a formal river leader or coach, please contact the trip organiser in advance - we can usually arrange something!  Otherwise you should be confident that your own ability matches the trip as planned.  If your trip is being led by someone - a more experienced paddler, river leader or a coach - it is your responsibility to mention any medical or other issues which may arise during the trip.
