Our regular weekly paddle sessions for members both new and experienced.  

If you're a newer member or would like some support on the water, please use the booking form below and one of our PAAs will meet you for a paddle.  If you're confident in your ability to paddle independently with the main group, please don't use the booking form - just turn up - and leave the spaces for those who need it.

 

Booking opens 2 weeks in advance.
Location Maidstone Canoe Club, Mill Meadow, St Peter's St, Maidstone, ME16 0SN

Disclaimer: our regular paddle sessions are peer paddles.  Support is generally available from more experienced paddlers or coaches, but you have to ask! Otherwise you should be confident that your own ability is appropriate for the journey you choose to do.  If your paddle is being led by someone - a more experienced paddler, river leader or a coach - it is your responsibility to mention any medical or other issues which may arise.
