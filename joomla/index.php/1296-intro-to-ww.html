Intro to WW!

Details
By Mark Corti

A huge thank-you to Bala Canoe Club who hosted us for this year's Intro to WW, and to Mary C & Paul F for adding immensely to the coaching.  A pretty full-on couple of days, with everyone who paddled making enormous improvements over the weekend, culminating in a successful descent of the Dee on the Sunday.  Congratulations all!  (Photos courtesy of Mary C & Richard R)
