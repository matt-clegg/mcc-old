Photos » 2013 » Stour Trip Sept. 13
Report Image

9 MCC paddlers spent the last weekend of August on the River Stour, in Suffolk. Some photos, and a few memory joggers, from the trip!!!

Campsite at Rushbanks Farm, near Nayland, Suffolk.

80-year "old dear" (you had to be there!).

An "old deer" (one of 80!). As I said, you had to be there!

Starburst sweets - an alternative to toasted marshmallows (but be careful, they get hot!).

A badger!!! We said we'd see one, and we did (but not this one).

Start point, Saturday - Sudbury.

Weir at Great Cornard lock.

Henny Street weir.

Pitmire/Daws weir.

Lunch at Lamarsh weir/fishpass.

Narrow stream at Bures Mill.

Wormingford weir.

Watch the paddle... Now you see it...

...now you don't...

...now you see it!!!!

Saturday finish point - campsite at Rushbanks Farm.

WWII re-enactors on the campsite.

Checking out Steve's shiny new hull!

Grass-hopper!

Wissington church; short walk from campsite.

Saturday evening.

Super-heated burgers!

Astronomy lesson part 1: shooting star (we saw several)..

Astronomy lesson part 2: using The Plough to find north (pole) star.

On-site catering for younger members!

Where did you get those hats?!

Sunday start point - Rushbanks farm landing stage.

The Stour, just downstream from campsite.

Wiston weir.

Wiston mill.

Tunnel under A134 near Nayland.

Nayland.
