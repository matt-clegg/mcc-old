From Saturday 17 September 2022
To Sunday 18 September 2022
      

This is a coaching weekend for those new to whitewater.  Paddling on moving water is the most fun you can have in a kayak!  We'll give you the foundation skills you need to move confidently around the river, breaking and and out of eddies, moving across the flow, and reading the river to find the best routes.

We'll be working on the fabulous River Dee, which is a lovely Grade 2 river in Llangollen, North Wales.  The variety of features, the gentle nature of the white water here  - and the cafe on the riverbank - make it probably the best training ground for moving water in the UK.  

This is suitable for anyone who has solid Explore Award skills or equivalent experience.  A good forward paddle stroke - with body rotation - and confidence in moving the kayak accurately on flat water are pre-requisites.  We will be using whitewater boats, not tourers.  If you haven't paddled moving water before, please contact Mark, Mary or Paul - we'll take you out on the Medway beforehand to look at some edging and other skills so you can get the most out of the weekend.

Accomodation details TBC but will likely be in a bunkhouse nearby.

See you there - it will be a huge amount of fun!

Note: any costs will be for accomodation only - all coaching is free at Maidstone Canoe Club.  The £10 below is a deposit to reserve your space and will be put towards your accomodation.
Contact Mark Corti, mark@maidstonecanoeclub.net
