Thursday 20 August 2015, 09:30 - 12:00
      

There’s nothing better on a summer’s day than messing about in boats! 

Join us on the river for a morning or afternoon splashing about in kayaks on the beautiful River Medway!  Fun, games and a lot of getting wet will make this the Summer Holiday activity the kids tell their friends about when they get back to school!  Run by our friendly and fully-qualified coaches, we’ll ensure that the kids have a fantastic but safe time on the river.
 
This session is open to children aged 8-11 who can swim unaided for at least 10 metres.  It costs just £10!
 
Your child should wear:
  • light clothing suitable for the weather - hopefully shorts & t-shirt, but anything other than jeans is fine.  These clothes will get wet!
  • old trainers, sandals, crocs etc. that you don’t mind getting wet
 
They will also need to bring:
  • a towel
  • change of clothes
  • a huge smile
 
We will provide 
  • boat
  • paddle
  • lifejacket
  • fully-qualified and CRB-checked coaches.
  • the best value day out of the Summer Holidays!
 
 

This session runs from 9:30am to 12:00pm.  Please arrive on time and pick your children up promptly at the end of the session!
