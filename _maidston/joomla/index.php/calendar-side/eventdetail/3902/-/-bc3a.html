Saturday 09 May 2015, 10:00 - 15:00
      

If you've ever fancied getting on the river in a canoe or kayak, but weren't sure where to start, this is for you!  Maidstone Canoe Club will be throwing open the doors of the boat shed for a day of water-based fun!  Come down and try your hand in a kayak, canoe, or (if you're a little nervous) on our super-stable Bell Boat!  

A fun-filled family day out - and best of all, it's FREE!

 

Questions?

  1. How much does it cost?  Nothing!  It's a totally free day out!
  2. What should I wear? Something you don't mind getting splashed!  T-shirt, tracksuit bottoms, shorts, old trainers - anything like this will be fine.  And please bring a towel - just in case!
  3. What age range is this for?  Ages 8-80!
  4. Anything else?  Please get in touch if you've got more questions and we'll do our best to help.
Location Maidstone Canoe Club, Mill Meadow, St Peter's St, Maidstone, ME16 0SN
Contact Bill Norman, bill.norman@maidstonecanoeclub.net
