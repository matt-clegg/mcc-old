Sunday 23 April 2017, 10:30 - 15:30
      

Tim Harlow of Maridian CC is organising the trip below and has invited anyone from Maidstone to join them, if you would like to go please get in touch with Tim to let him know   Tim Harlow <tjharlow@hotmail.com>;

we are doing The Kentish Stour this Sunday the 23rd April.

Meet at Grove Ferry Car Park (CT3 4BP) 10.15.
There is a £2 parking charge, toilets and changing rooms.
Launch by 10.30.
HW Grove Ferry 12:56
Paddle 4 miles to arrive at Fordwich 12:15
We will either have lunch in The Fordwich Arms if numbers are small or packed lunch if higher. 
I predict tide will turn during this time.
Leave 13:30
Return to Grove Ferry 15:30
Leave 16:00

Full details at:-

http://www.meridiancanoeclub.com/phpBB3/viewtopic.php?f=4&t=1559

Could you let me know numbers?

The pub gets busy at lunch time so a packed lunch is probably a better idea.

Tim Harlow
