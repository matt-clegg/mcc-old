Peer Sea Paddling Trip to Lefkada and Meganisi island, Greece

06 09 October 2022

Schedule:

Thursday 6 Oct:

Arrival day. We settled into our accommodation at the sea shore. Our base will be at Dessimi and every day we will start and finish our kayaking trips there. In the evenings we can visit some nice taverns or prepare our own dinner on a barbeque by the beach.

 

Friday 7 Oct:

First kayaking day. Possible itinerary: Dessimi Meganisi island. Lunch at a tavern on the island. Return to Dessimi

Saturday 8 Oct:

Second kayaking day. Possible itinerary: We head North by the Scorpio, Scorpidi, Maduri islands. We head inside the large bay of Nidri. Lunch at a tavern in Gheni. Return to Dessimi.

 

Sunday 9 Oct:  

Third kayaking day. Possible itinerary: we head south towards the deep underwater caves of Lefcas. We could go under the rocks with the kayaks and explore some caverns.
We will enter the deep bays of Micros Gialos or Sivota. We will have lunch in a tavern near the hotel and after that we will have time to shower and pack for our flight home.


Around Lefkas there are several possible routes for kayaking, depending on the weather, and the sea, as well as the abilities and wishes of the group participants. We could visit some interesting caverns, desolate beaches, rock walls, and beautiful bays. Part of the pleasure is in visiting different taverns with the kayaks for lunch and for refreshing drinks.

Cost of the trip:

Option 1 - Camping - 195
A tent could be provided by the club, but you have to bring your own sleeping bag and mat. Camping - non electric green tent pitch with all facilities.

Option 2 - Shared hotel room - 285
You will be suited in a double or triple hotel room.

Option 3 - Private hotel room - 360

What is included in the price is the option you choose plus:

- All local fees.
- Boats (tandem kayaks) + all gear required for paddling such as, paddles, buoyancy aids and dry bags.
- Trip leader/guide
- The shuttles from and to Aktion International Airport.

Additional costs that everyone must cover:

- Flights tickets - please make sure that you booked yourself a tickets for these flights, they are chosen to fit to our travel plan:

- Food and drinks at the venues
- Drinks and Snacks
- Travel insurance
- All paperwork needed for travel to greece.
- Suitable clothing for this paddling such as:
Swimming suits or some fast drying layer
Windstopper or waterproof jacket.
Swimming shoes or flip flops.
Spare set of dry clothes and shoes for our breaks for dinner.

 

 If you are interested in joining that trip please contact me for more information.

 
Location Greece
Contact georgi@hebar.co.uk

Disclaimer: all our trips are run by club members like you, and unless specifically described otherwise are peer paddles.  If you think that you would benefit from the support of a formal river leader or coach, please contact the trip organiser in advance - we can usually arrange something!  Otherwise you should be confident that your own ability matches the trip as planned.  If your trip is being led by someone - a more experienced paddler, river leader or a coach - it is your responsibility to mention any medical or other issues which may arise during the trip.
