Saturday 10 December 2016, 19:00
      

Maidstone Canoe Club Christmas Party will take place on

Saturday 10th December at the Clubhouse.

Starting at 7.00pm.

Tickets are just £5.00 per person and this includes a buffet supper.

Please bring your own drinks.

The club will be transformed into Christmas Wonderland, or something like it, and we will have the best Christmas music ever.

There will be a raffle with a fabulous prize. If you have something you can donate for the raffle, so there are a few more prizes, it would be much appreciated.

Tickets can be purchased from the club website or directly from Geoff. You will need to buy your ticket by Thursday 1st December to give us time to order the food. (These are not real tickets - more like a list!)
Location At the Clubhouse
Contact niki@maidstonecanoeclub.net
