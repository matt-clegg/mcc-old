Yalding to Club mini-tour - Sunday 27th October

Details
By Steve Chinn

In addition to the details already published, note that we should be able to use the canoe pass at Teston Lock on this trip. With rain forecast for the next few days there could be some flow on the river. If you have a helmet, bring it along (or borrow a club one), particularly if you want to run the canoe pass, or the fish steps at East Farleigh.

Clocks as Yalding

Details

The clocks go back on Sunday, so take advantage of the extra hour and come for a paddle down from Yalding. Meet at the Club at 8 - see calendar for details.

Elmbridge Hasler

Details
By Steve Chinn

The club crews had a good day at Elmbridge on Sunday (29th Sept). All our adult/junior ‘K’ crews were placed either 1st, 2nd or 3rd in their respective divisions. And young Joel came a respectable 4th in the U10 lightnings. Results are here, and there are lots of photos here and here.

Rowing Races Sunday 6th October

There will be no general paddling at the club next Sunday 6th October because of the rowing races. There will be an inter club paddle form Oare Slipway, details on the calendar, all welcome.
