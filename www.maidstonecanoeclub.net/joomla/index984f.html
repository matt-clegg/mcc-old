Photos to promote new canoe trails

Details
By Mark Corti

Kent County Council are trying to promote canoe trails in Kent, and are looking for some good photos to help with their promotion.  They're particularly interested in people, wildlife or action shots in the following locations:

  • River Medway - Tonbridge to Allington
  • Royal Military Canal
  • Pegwell Bay and Stour (Ramsgate to Sandwich to Canterbury)

Please contact Steve Law of the Explore Kent team on stephen.law@kent.gov.uk if you are able to help
