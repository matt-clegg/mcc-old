First Aid Help

Details
By Mark Corti

The Club could really use your help!  We currently don't have a first aid officer.  The role can be as much or as little as you want - as a minimum, keeping the first aid kit topped up, but if you wanted to expand the role to arranging (or running) first aid training or anything else, then it's yours to run with!  If you've a background in healthcare - nurse, paramedic, brain surgeon, St John's ambulance trainer, etc. - then even better!  

Please contact Catherine D - secretary@maidstonecanoeclub.net - to chat about what's involved.

Floating Pennywort removal 15 November

Details
By Catherine Dacey

The Medway Countryside partnership are running a work party to remove floating pennywort, a non native invasive species, from the Medway at Yalding.

They are looking for volunteers in canoes or kayaks to rake and collect the pennywort.

If you can help out, please contact Catherine Dacey (secretary@maidstonecanoeclub.net) by 7th November so we can arrange transport of boats and advise MCP of our numbers.

There will be tea/ coffee and cake provided along with alcohol gel.
