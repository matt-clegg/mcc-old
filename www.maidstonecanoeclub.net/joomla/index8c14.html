Boat jousting

Details
By Mark Corti

By an astonishing coincidence, our pool session next week falls on the 502nd anniversary of the jousting contest and meeting between Henry VIII & Francis I at the Field of the Cloth of Gold near Calais.  We will - of course - be celebrating this important occasion at the pool session.  See you there!  (Plate armour & horses will not permitted in the pool)

First Aid

Details
By Mark Corti

We've got a first aid course running on July 16th, focussing on skills useful on the water and off.  If you haven't done a first aid course for a while, it's definitely worth getting an update - things have moved on, and it's good to have it fresh in your mind if you do ever need it.

This 8-hour course is approved by British Canoeing for coaching and river leading qualifications.
