Learn to Canoe!

Details
By Mark Corti

We've still got spaces on our Canoeing Beginner Course on the 19th & 26th June.  This is a rare opportunity to learn to paddle a Canadian-style open canoe - we typically only run one of these each year!  These boats are fantastic for couples, families, bushcraft and wilderness paddling, as well as a great craft for those who don't like the enclosed feel of a kayak.  Spaces are strictly limited.

Course feedback

Details
By Mark Corti

I was looking through the feedback from our beginner courses held over the last few years, and I wanted to share some of it with you.  The top three responses to the question "What did you enjoy most about the course?" were

  1. The patience of the coaches
  2. How friendly everyone was at the Club
  3. The enthusiasm from everyone on the water

Well done everyone - give yourself a pat on the back this Monday afternoon!
