Latest News

Kent and beyond

Details
By Mark Corti

Beult. The Swale. Stour. Hammer Stream. Rother. And there are dozens more besides just in Kent!

if you'd like to stretch your paddling legs but aren't sure where to start, then sign up for the Explore or Touring Awards running in the next few weeks. They'll give you the knowledge and confidence you need to paddle further afield - and teach you some skills to boot!  See the calendar for dates and to book. 

New course dates released

Details
By Mark Corti

As Ratty famously said in The Wind in the Willows, there is nothing - absolutely nothing - half so much worth doing as simply messing about in boats!  That goes double when the weather is as gorgeous as it has been for the last few weeks.

We've just added more beginner course dates in July & August - check the website for details and to book online!

Our legendary Junior Paddle Club is also going from strength to strength, with new-paddler inductions every Tuesday evening - again, booking is open online!

We look forward to seeing you on the river!

Rescue practice

Details
By Mark Corti

Pool sessions are a great place to practice your capsizes, learn to rescue yourself and others, and maybe learn some new skills so you can be more adventurous with your paddling!  There's plenty of help available from coaches and experienced paddlers, just ask :-). The next pool session is June 20th - please book online.  

Islands of the Medway Estuary

Details
By Paul William Lane

Guildhall Rochester Talks Islands of the Medway Estuary by Adam Taylor a keen Canoest. Friday 21st July 2023 7.30 - 8.30pm. It's free to attend but I need to know numbers - please send me an email. A donation is asked to cover tea, coffee and biscuits.
