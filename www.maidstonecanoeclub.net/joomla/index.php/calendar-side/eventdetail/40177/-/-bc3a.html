Kayaking Trip in Lycia

Türkiye’s Azure Coast

17 – 24 June 2023

The dates may shift by a day, depending on flight availability.

Day 1:

Flight to Antalya. Prearranged transfer form Antalya to Olympos in a van. Accommodation in treehouses and bungalows.

Day 2:

A Day at Olympus. Time to view the ancient town; Beach time; Free time; Optional visit to the eternal fires of Chimaera. Accommodation in the bungalows.

Day 3:

Transfer to Demre. Our kayaking tour starts in the town vicinity. Kayaking to
Kekova. Overnight at Uçağız.

Day 4:

Long kayaking day in Kekova. Transfer to Kaş and accommodation there.

Day 5:

Kayaking in Kaş. Overnight in Kaş.

Day 6:

Sightseeing day. Visit Xanthos and Saklıkent (The Lost City), transfer to Kayakoy. Visit Kayakoy and overnight there.

Day 7:

Paddle in Oludeniz (The Blue Lagoon) and the Butterfly Valley. Overnight in Kayakoy. Final party.

Day 8:

Transfer to Antalya and flight home.

In case of bad weather and rough seas, the kayaking itinerary may be altered. Some
of the sightseeing destination may be swapped or replaced.

Participation cost:

650 GBP

This does not include airfare and taxes. Below you will find a detailed description what is included in this cost and what not.

What is included in the participation cost:

All land transfers described in the programme; all sightseeing visits; all accommodation; some of the meals at some accommodations; the sea kayaking trips; specialised kayaking gear: kayaks, paddles, BA’s; the compulsory local kayaking and tour guides; a tour coordinator from the club.At Olympus we will have  breakfasts and dinners included. We expect that most accommodations will include breakfasts. After we have the exact group size and members, we will determine the exact accommodation venues and details about what meals can be included.

What is NOT covered by the participation cost:

Flight tickets and taxes; insurance; meals that are not already specified as included; all drinks; entrance fees to sightseeing sites; the visit to Chimaera; personal gear and equipment, proper clothing, footwear, headwear, sunscreen etc.

Accommodation:

The lodging will not be luxurious, but we will try to select it to be nice and comfortable. Accommodation will be in rooms for 2 and 3 people. Single occupancy
would cost extra. We will be staying in some cosy family-run hotels and guesthouses. In Olympos we will be staying in special tree-house complexes. Olympos is a touristy village with a hippie vibe. We will be staying in a closed resort of sorts, with green gardens, treehouses, bungalows, tea and dining area.

Food:

Breakfast and some of the dinners will be at the places we stay for the night. The local cuisine is various and attractive. We will have the chance to visit some
interesting local pubs and restaurants. Lunches will be decided on the spot, according to availability. 

If someone has dietary restrictions: allergies, special diets like vegetarian, vegan, etc. it is compulsory to inform in advance, so that the host can be prepared.

Paddling:

The kayaking tours are at sea, with incredibly beautiful and various scenery. We will
have the chance to visit historical remains, bays and beaches. The length of the
tours will be different day by day and will be determined according to the conditions
of the sea and the group.

If you want to come along or  if you need more information contact Georgi: georgi@hebar.co.uk

Disclaimer: all our trips are run by club members like you, and unless specifically described otherwise are peer paddles.  If you think that you would benefit from the support of a formal river leader or coach, please contact the trip organiser in advance - we can usually arrange something!  Otherwise you should be confident that your own ability matches the trip as planned.  If your trip is being led by someone - a more experienced paddler, river leader or a coach - it is your responsibility to mention any medical or other issues which may arise during the trip.
