Sunday 25 September 2016, 09:30 - 16:30
      

What is it?

This is a two day training and assessment course, aiming for the participants to try and acheive their 3 Star Touring Award. This is an ideal course for anyone wishing:

  • To gain their 3 Star without having to paddle on moderate water
  • To learn more about journeying and paddling on trips
  • To gain their 3 Star without having to be able to complete an eskimo roll

The course will cover:

  • Lifting, carrying and launching
  • Efficient paddling forwards, backwards and turning
  • Moving sideways more effectively
  • Support strokes, preventing a capsize
  • Dealing with the environment; wind, current etc
  • Peer and self rescues
  • Towing
  • Risk management and awareness of others
  • Other theory such as: equipment, first aid, access, planning, navigation, etiquette etc.

the course will run on Sunday 4th 0930 to 1630 traning and Sunday 25th 0930 to 1630 assessment  at the club and yalling

Pre-requisites

For the training alone participants must either hold the 2 Star award or be at that level and be a minimum age of 14. If you wish to pass the course over the weekend you will need to have evidence of at least 3 different flat-water journeys of about 3 hours duration (10km) each with at least one of these in an open water environment. And also provide evidence of at least one session of about 3 hours duration in an alternative discipline.
