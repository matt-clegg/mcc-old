From Friday 14 September 2018 -  08:00
To Sunday 16 September 2018 - 17:00
      

Touring trip to South Wales.  This will be centred on the river Wye and aimed at Touring paddlers but everyone looking for grade 1/2rivers. Are welcome
Saturday day 1 Whitney - Brdwardilne
Sunday day 2 Brdwardine - Bycross

 Please bring with you the following

Lunch or you can buy it there.

Camping kit, Tent

Paddle kit

Camping fee £8.00 per adult per night (£5 for under 12 years)

Money for pub  

Food for BBQ Frday night Bring own drink 

Breakfast menu – your choice of:

Breakfast bap filled with your choice of locally sourced bacon, sausage, free range egg, fresh tomato and chestnut mushrooms plus unlimited selection of beverages incl. green tea, tea, fruit tea, hot chocolate and coffee – delivered to your tent - £5.00 each.

Saturday options

Pub Menu to Follow will need orders and number by Monday 21st May 2018  Fully loaded BBQ option - £15.00 per head to include:

Unlimited burger buns with variety of topping choices and choice of 100% Beef burger, chicken breast or grilled Halloumi (sheep’s milk cheese) PLUS grilled fish/prawns Plus 2 BBQ chicken drumsticks PLUS stuffed mushrooms and stuffed jacket potatoes PLUS variety of mixed salads, dips and toppings and more if you have any special diet requirements let me kown

 Bring own drink  Can do both options

 
 
Location Whitney Bridge Toll Bridge Cottage Whitney-on-Wye Herefordshire HR3 6EW
Contact lee@maidstonecanoeclub.net

Disclaimer: all our trips are run by club members like you, and unless specifically described otherwise are peer paddles.  If you think that you would benefit from the support of a formal river leader or coach, please contact the trip organiser in advance - we can usually arrange something!  Otherwise you should be confident that your own ability matches the trip as planned.  If your trip is being led by someone - a more experienced paddler, river leader or a coach - it is your responsibility to mention any medical or other issues which may arise during the trip.
