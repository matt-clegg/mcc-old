Saturday 19 July 2014, 20:00 - 21:30
      

Cost: £6 for members, £8 for non-members.  Please bring your membership card with you!

Please ensure that your boat is clean inside and out. Boats will be inspected prior to entry, and unclean boats will be turned away.

Club boats can be used for these sessions, but you'll need to make arrangements for getting one up there.  Boats must be returned by Sunday morning.  Please contact Richard Clark, the general paddling Bosun, if you would like to take a club boat. Boat cleaning at the club from 16:00 on the day for the pool sessioon

No seal launching - please keep the poolside clean.

Please respect the pool or we will lose it!
Location Larkfield Leisure Centre, New Hythe Lane, Larkfield, Kent, ME20 6RH
