Friday 27 May 2016, 20:00
To Saturday 04 June 2016
      

        

This year the plan is to base the week around the islands of Ulva and Iona on the west coast of Mull, with the possibility of going out to Staffa, the location of Fingal's Cave if the weather is calm. We will take the first ferry on Saturday 28th May from Oban to Craignure and then drive across Mull to launch on Loch Na Keal and head for the first wild campsite on Ulva. Later in the week we will possibly head back to the cars and drive down towards Iona for another launch, all weather dependent. Please let me know if you would like to go, there are a couple of sea boats in the club that can be used for this but it will be first come first served.    

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
Location Mull
Contact geofforford@gmail.com

Disclaimer: all our trips are run by club members like you, and unless specifically described otherwise are peer paddles.  If you think that you would benefit from the support of a formal river leader or coach, please contact the trip organiser in advance - we can usually arrange something!  Otherwise you should be confident that your own ability matches the trip as planned.  If your trip is being led by someone - a more experienced paddler, river leader or a coach - it is your responsibility to mention any medical or other issues which may arise during the trip.
