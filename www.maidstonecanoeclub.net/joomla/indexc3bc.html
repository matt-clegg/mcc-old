Giving back?

Details
By Mark Corti

The wonderful thing about Maidstone Canoe Club is how much people are prepared to help each other.  All of us have, at some time, been taken down the river - the Medway or elsewhere - by more-experienced paddlers who have given up their time to make sure we're having fun.

With the beginner courses starting in less than two weeks, we're going to have an influx of newer paddlers - excited, eager to learn and have fun, but perhaps a little unsure.  We need helpers to be a friendly face, to make sure they're safe on the water and introduce them to new people.  We need you!

The Club's Paddlesport Activity Assistant program has been a huge success, and many of you reading this probably owe at least some of your paddling time to those wonderful people!  You can join them - even if you just help out once or twice a year, it makes a massive difference to our new paddlers and to the Club.

Please contact Jacob and he'll help you through the (short!) training paddle.  Thank you!

Details

You wait for ages, and then two come along at once!  We've got two different rescue sessions coming up next month:
